And sometimes it’s really hard to identify how they’re doing it I eventually started calling these team members catalysts, because they’re like chemical catalysts that make reactions go faster or more efficiently or happen more readily at lower temperatures, but don’t themselves get used up in it.

They just by being there, things go more smoothly. These are people who make teams work better or faster or more efficiently, in addition to their — whatever their own — work is.
