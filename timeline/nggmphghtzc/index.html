Well, guess what? Problem one: This is no longer an escort quest. This is just a companion acquisition quest.

At this point I usually tell junior designers: welcome to game design, because there is really no good solution to this. You’re going to have to pick all the problems I just mentioned. You’re going to have to pick the solution that you hate the least, or that you think will annoy your player base the least, or that you think fits best with your uh design pillars, including quest pillars and exploration pillars and whatever pillars you had for companions.
