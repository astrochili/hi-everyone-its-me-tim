Which brings me to the last question about multiplayer — and I’ve answered this before and I don’t think my answer would change: making Fallout. Real time, even though a lot of people think, hey, it’s real time, now let’s add multiplayer — no, I view Fallout, the setting, as a desolate, uncaring world where the choices that the player makes changes things. Multiplayer ruins all of that.

It doesn’t feel desolate when there’s a bunch of Vault suit wearing people, bunny, hopping around and talking to you and laughing and, you know, telling you to get good.
