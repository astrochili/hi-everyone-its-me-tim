“Hi everyone. It’s me, Tim.”

That is how most videos on Timothy Cain’s channel begin. Cain’s contribution to game development, and especially to computer RPGs, is hard to overstate, and the channel is full of something increasingly rare: calm, generous, experience-driven reflections from someone who spent decades building games and thinking carefully about how they are made.

He speaks candidly about design, programming, production, writing, leadership, studio history, and the day-to-day realities of making games. Many of these videos are worth revisiting, searching, and reading slowly.

This site has no official connection to Tim Cain. Its purpose is simple: to make the channel easier to browse as a text archive, with transcript pages, topic tags, and links between related videos.

It is useful for browsing, searching, and slow reading, but it is not an official site, not a perfect transcript source, and not a replacement for the original videos.

Disclaimer

Everything here is built from subtitle files and AI-assisted cleanup, so errors remain inevitable. Names can be wrong, punctuation can drift, sentences can come out awkwardly, and some passages may still misrepresent what was actually said.

Please treat this as a reading aid rather than an authoritative source, and defer to the original videos whenever accuracy matters. If you want the original source material, use the embedded video or the direct YouTube link on each video page.
